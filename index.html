Resume

As a seasoned marketing leader, I've discovered a passion for nurturing startups and flourishing together. Guiding brands as we learn to crawl and then learn to walk, and finally run is where I find my stride. I've been fortunate to play a role in the growth stories of several amazing companies. Not without a good dose of failures in between, certainly. My journey has taken me through diverse accountabilities, including market research, content creation, and brand development. Using data-driven insights, I've crafted campaigns that resonate with audiences, drive engagement, and conversions.

Yet, it's the pursuit of building virtuous global brands that fuels my drive. I believe in the power of impactful storytelling, ethical practices, and meaningful connections. I've had the privilege of witnessing the ripple effects that conscious branding can have on not only businesses, but also the communities we impact.

From random tinkering in the digital landscape of the '90s to the strategic campaigns of today, every step of my journey has equipped me with insights, resilience, and a thirst for continuous learning.

As I stand at this juncture, my goal is clear: to contribute to building a better world, one brand at a time.

This short collection of experiences showcases my dedication, adaptability, and passion for growth.
